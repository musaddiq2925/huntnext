Our Tech Teams

Engineering

See open roles

Whether you are working on developing search and recommendation algorithms or finding ways to accelerate the company's growth, you will have the chance to make a real impact. At HuntNext, the Engineering team is constantly pushing the boundaries and finding new ways to bring the magic of travel to people all over the world.

One of the major problems we are working to solve is how to make it easy for our users to discover and book activities, even when they are in a destination that is new to them. Another challenge is to build solutions that can help personalize travel experiences. Additionally, we are always looking for ways to improve the efficiency and reliability of our booking process, so that our users can have confidence in their plans and have a stress-free vacation.

One thing in particular that I love about HuntNext is the culture. In Zurich it's easy to casually bump into each other at the office, meet new people, and have random chats about life and work. In fact, it was only since starting this job that I realized how much I missed this social element during the pandemic when we were all working from home.

Susanna Ferrari
Engineering Manager, Marketing Optimization and Visuals Team

How We Learn

Hackdays

Two-day hackathons where you can work on a project of your choice with colleagues in varying domains across the organization.

They occur every 2 months and provide an opportunity to try something new, build something impactful, and/or meet new people. Here you can learn, build, ship, and have a lot of fun.

Impact:
  • 171 Ideas pitched
  • 132 Projects executed

Guilds

Guilds at HuntNext — for example Data Engineering — unite people across teams who share the same passions. By bringing together those in similar roles, guilds build bridges across silos. The result? A continuous learning culture that amplifies our collective impact.

Book clubs

Books are a great source of learning and relearning — we love them at HuntNext. If you do too, you can join groups like the UX Book Club and Technical Book Club.

Tech lunches

Our teams come together to share technical learnings every Thursday.
